Features

Unique Artistic Design: Polar Skate Co. is known for its distinctive graphics, often inspired by contemporary art, photography, and a DIY aesthetic. The brand collaborates with artists like Jacob Ovgren and Emile Laurent, adding a unique artistic touch to their products.


How to works

Polar Skate Co. combines unique artistic design, high-quality materials, and a skate team to create products that resonate with skate culture and streetwear enthusiasts.


pricing

Polar Skate Co. offers boards typically priced between €60-€100, clothing like t-shirts, hoodies, and jackets from €40-€150, and accessories such as hats and socks from €20-€50; limited editions and collaborations may cost more.
